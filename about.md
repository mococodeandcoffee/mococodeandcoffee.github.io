Code & Coffee is a coding meetup for any dev-curious, aspiring, or professional developer to work, hack on your project, or share knowledge with cool, inclusive people. All skill levels are welcome. Bring your laptop!

The site represents the Montgomery County, MD chapter. If you are interested in finding other chapters, check out the [main Code & Coffee website]({{ site.links.codeandcoffee }}){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.

## Our Principles

Our community centers around three core values:

**Belonging**, **Safety**, and **Trust**

Please report harassment to an organizer.

## For Developers

As a software developer, you might come to a meetup to:

- Look for a better job
- Meet developers and make friends
- Validate the feasibility of an ambitious side project
- Get help with a coding blocker or homework
- Gain insight into a company you're interviewing for
- Learn about other professions involving code
- Join a vibrant online Discord community
- Hang out, sip coffee, and spend time with friendly people

## For Tech Companies

We're currently available for sponsorships and partnerships! We may be helpful in areas such as developer relations, brand visibility, product feedback, user interviews, recruiting, lead generation, and community engagement.
